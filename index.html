About PricePilot.ie

Built for Irish shoppers.
By an Irish shopper.

What is PricePilot.ie?

PricePilot.ie is a free price comparison tool that helps Irish shoppers find the cheapest price across all six Amazon marketplaces in Europe — Ireland, UK, Germany, France, Italy and Spain. We show the cheapest total cost including delivery to Ireland.

Why did we build this?

Ireland consistently pays more for consumer goods. Amazon.ie only launched in 2025 and prices are often higher than other European stores for the exact same product. PricePilot was built so Irish shoppers can make genuinely informed decisions.
