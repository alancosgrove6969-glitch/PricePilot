About PricePilot.ie

Built for Irish shoppers.
By an Irish shopper.

What is PricePilot.ie?

PricePilot.ie is a free price comparison tool that helps Irish shoppers find the cheapest price for products across all six Amazon marketplaces in Europe — Ireland, UK, Germany, France, Italy and Spain. We show not just the cheapest price, but the cheapest total cost including delivery to Ireland.

Why did we build this?

Ireland consistently ranks as one of the most expensive countries in Europe for consumer goods. Amazon.ie only launched in early 2025, and prices are often higher than other European Amazon stores for the exact same product. We built PricePilot so Irish shoppers can make genuinely informed decisions.

Affiliate Disclosure

PricePilot.ie participates in the Amazon EU Associates Programme. When you click a link and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission at absolutely no extra cost to you. Our commission never influences which store we recommend — sorting is always based purely on total delivered price.
